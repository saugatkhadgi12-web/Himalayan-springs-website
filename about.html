About us

Himalayan Spring Water Incorporated

We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the top quality mineral water producers in Nepal by Ultra-modern Plant using latest American Technology. We at Himalayan Spring Water Inc; have taken TRIPLE safety to provide you the safest and the healthiest water by using following THREE processes together:

Reverse Osmosis

Reverse osmosis is a water purification process that uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities, contaminants, and dissolved salts by forcing water through the membrane under pressure.

Ozonation

Ozonation is a water treatment process that disinfects and removes contaminants by injecting ozone gas, a powerful oxidant, which breaks down bacteria, viruses, and organic materials.

Ultraviolet Treatment

Ultraviolet (UV) treatment is a water purification method that uses UV light to destroy harmful microorganisms by disrupting their DNA, effectively disinfecting the water without chemicals.

We derive our water from a Mountain Spring located at our factory premises itself. Our plant and the Mountain Spring is located right after the dense forest of Chandragiri Range of Mountains at Chhaikhel, Kathmandu, few kilometers before Goddess Dakshinkali. There is no human settlement or any pollution in between our Mountain Spring and the dense forest. Therefore our source of water is completely free from pollution by toxic waste, human pollution, chemical pollution, industrial, or even locomotive pollution. Thus 100% safety to your health is our guarantee.
